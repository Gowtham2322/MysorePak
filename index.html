Mysore pak

    Gram flour
     Sugar
     Ghee

The classic Mysore Pak recipe typically uses a 1:1:1 ratio of gram flour, sugar, and ghee. For example, if using 1 cup of gram flour, you would also use 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of ghee.
